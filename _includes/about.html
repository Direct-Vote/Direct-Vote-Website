What is directVote?

directVote is a campaign to bring the British political system into the 21st century by allowing voters a bypass the ruling parties and vote directly on the issues that matter via mobile phones and social media. We intend to field candidates in all 650 English, Scottish, Welsh and Northern Ireland constituencies in the 2020 Election.

We believe the Conservatives, Labour, Lib Dems and other parties have all proven themselves to be incapable of representing the mainstream view of the population. They are self-serving, self-interested parties who rule with scant regard for the people they pretend to represent. directVote aims to replace the current party system with a system where the every member of the electorate can vote directly on the every issue raised in parliament via mobile and social technology.
