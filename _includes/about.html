What is directVote?

directVote is a campaign to bring the British political system into the 21st century by enabling voting in real-time via mobile phones and social media.

We aim to replace the current party system with one where every voter can vote directly on all the issues, not just at election time every five years.

We intend to field candidates in all 650 English, Scottish, Welsh and Northern Irish constituencies in the 2020 Election. Join us and be one of them.
